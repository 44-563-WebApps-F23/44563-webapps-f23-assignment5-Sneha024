Cities row breaks first

As we decrease the screen size, you'll observe that the city names will be the first to shift to a new line. Bootstrap's responsive design adapts the column widths according to the screen's size. On smaller screens, the columns labeled with "col-md" will arrange themselves vertically, while the columns labeled with "col-sm-2" will continue to stay side by side horizontally for a longer period. Hence the content adjusts effectively to varying screen sizes due to bootstrap.
